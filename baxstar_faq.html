Fishing & Lakes

Walleye is the primary target and what Detroit Lakes, Minnesota is known for. We also target largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, northern pike, perch, and crappie depending on season and what you want to catch. Brady adjusts the plan based on what's biting — not what's easiest.
Baxstar Fishing has access to 100+ lakes in the Detroit Lakes, Minnesota area. Primary waters include Detroit Lake, Lake Sallie, Lake Melissa, Pelican Lake, the Cormorant Lakes chain, and Lida. Brady selects the best lake for conditions — not just the closest one. That local knowledge is the whole point.
Detroit Lakes fishes well year-round. Walleye: strong from the May opener through late June, peaks again in July around weed edges, produces consistently through October. Bass: best June through August. Ice fishing: walleye, perch, and crappie from December through February. Brady adapts strategy — every season has a best approach.
Your Minnesota fishing license is included with every guided trip. You do not need to purchase one separately. Just show up ready to fish.
