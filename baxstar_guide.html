Your Guide

Brady Baxter

Owner & Head Guide — Baxstar Fishing

Brady Baxter has spent more than 30 years fishing the Detroit Lakes region and over seven years guiding professionally. With five Master Angler awards, a Forbes feature, more than 20 TV appearances, and clients who rebook before they leave the dock, Brady has earned his reputation as one of the area's most trusted voices on walleye, bass, pike, and panfish. That same local knowledge is why he authors the weekly Detroit Lakes area fishing report.

He does not rely on one lake, one pattern, or one technique. With access to more than 400 lakes within 25 miles of Detroit Lakes and a lifetime of experience across every season and condition, Brady reads the water the way most people read a room — quickly, accurately, and with a clear plan.
