Questions

BEFORE YOU BOOK.

Everything you need to know about your trip. Still have questions? Call or text Brady directly — he responds personally.

What should I bring? +

Just yourself, weather-appropriate clothing, sunscreen, and a cooler for beverages. All fishing equipment, bait, tackle, and safety gear is provided. Brady runs a fully outfitted operation — you don't need to bring a single piece of fishing gear.

Do I need a fishing license? +

Yes — Minnesota requires a valid fishing license for anyone 16 and older. You can purchase one online at the Minnesota DNR website in about two minutes. Brady can walk you through it if you need help.

How many people can fish per boat? +

Each boat accommodates up to 4 guests comfortably. For larger groups, Brady coordinates multiple boats with additional guides so everyone gets the full experience. Corporate groups and family reunions welcome.

What fish species can we target? +

Walleye, largemouth and smallmouth bass, northern pike, musky, crappie, and panfish. Brady selects the best species and lake based on current conditions, time of year, and your preferences.

What if the weather is bad? +

Light rain? Some of the best fishing days. Dangerous conditions like lightning or severe storms? Brady will work with you to reschedule at no charge. Safety is never compromised.

Is this good for beginners? +

Absolutely. Brady adjusts every trip to your experience level. First-timers get hands-on instruction and patient coaching. Experienced anglers get advanced techniques and pushed to new water. Everyone catches fish.
