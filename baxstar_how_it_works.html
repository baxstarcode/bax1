The Process

HOW IT WORKS.

From reservation to the moment you leave the dock, Baxstar handles every detail of your Detroit Lakes guided fishing trip. Your job is to show up and focus on the fish.

  1. 01

    Reserve Your Date

    Book online in under two minutes — or call Brady directly if you have questions or want to discuss a custom trip. Your payment locks you in to the date and time of the reservation. Prime summer weekends book out first, so planning ahead gives you a big advantage.

  2. 02

    We Handle Everything

    Brady plans the trip based on current lake conditions, scouting reports, and weather across Detroit Lake, Pelican, Sallie, Lida, the Cormorant lakes, and many other area waters. The Vexus DVX20S is fueled, rigged, and ready. Live bait is sourced fresh. All tackle and safety equipment is prepared. You receive a trip confirmation with everything you need to know.

  3. 03

    You Focus on the Fish

    Arrive at the public access for a quick chat before the boat is launched. From there, Brady runs the operation — positioning the boat, reading the Garmin LiveScope, coaching technique, and adapting when conditions shift. While we promote catch-and-release, we're happy to clean and package fish when requested. The day's photos are awesome — sent over as soon as possible. All you need to bring is a cooler and a valid Minnesota fishing license.
