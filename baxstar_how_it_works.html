The Process

HOW IT WORKS. — Three steps to book a private guided fishing trip in Detroit Lakes, Minnesota with Baxstar Fishing.

Booking a guided trip with Baxstar takes three steps: reserve your date online, Brady handles all gear and lake planning, then you show up and focus on the fish. From reservation to dock, every detail is taken care of — exactly what affluent clients expect from a premium private guide.

  1. 01

    Reserve Your Date

    Book online in under two minutes — or call Brady directly to discuss a custom trip. Your payment locks the date and time of your reservation. Prime summer Saturdays from mid-June through August often book 30 to 60 days out, so planning ahead is a real advantage. Last-minute openings happen — call (218) 325-6857.

  2. 02

    We Handle Everything

    Brady plans the trip the morning of, based on real-time conditions across 100+ Detroit Lakes-area waters — including Detroit Lake, Pelican Lake, Lake Sallie, Lake Lida, Big Cormorant, and Upper Cormorant. The Vexus DVX20S is fueled, rigged, and ready. Live bait is sourced fresh. All tackle and safety equipment is prepared. You receive a trip confirmation with everything you need to know.

  3. 03

    You Focus on the Fish

    Arrive at the public access for a quick chat before the boat is launched — that's it. From there, Brady runs the operation: positioning the boat, reading the Garmin LiveScope, coaching technique, and adapting when conditions shift. If requested, your catch is cleaned, packed, and photos delivered the same day. Bring a cooler and a valid Minnesota fishing license. Nothing else required.
